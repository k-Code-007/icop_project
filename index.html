    • Technology

    ICOP technology assumes complete autonomy.
    Any modern smartphone (Android or iPhone) with a conventional video camera can be used. Exotic technologies such as Smart Light and Lidar are not used.

    Smartphone installation is arbitrary.
    It is only required to ensure the visibility of the road. No preparatory procedures are required. Moderate Vibration is acceptable and does not significantly affect measurement performance.

    ICOP takes care of smartphone resources.
    The processor load is low, which allows you to use your smartphone at the same time for any other purpose, in particular for the navigation application. Only the data of the detected violation is stored in the memory.

    All identified violations are georeferenced, which allows you to determine the speed limit on a given section, as well as the action of the signs "Give way", "Stop", etc. The system detects sudden braking or a sharp change in the direction of movement and determines the car of the intruder, which caused it. Controlled the inclusion of sidelights and traffic signals.

    About

    ICOP Engineering technologies, based on the latest advances in AI and Computer Vision, allow even an ordinary smartphone to be turned into an effective means of quantitative monitoring of the traffic situation in real-time. People have long learned to control the speed of their own cars. But now, with ICOP technologies, you can determine the speed of all oncoming and advancing vehicles. And not only speed!
